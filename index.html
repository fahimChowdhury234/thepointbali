About the point

Welcome to The Point, boasting an exclusive cliff top location with uninterrupted sunset and ocean views, looking out to the glittering coastline of Bali and the majestic Mount Agung.

The Point was designed to provide a private getaway for couples or groups of up to 8 looking for a truly relaxing stay. All four large suites are fully appointed with an infinity pool to maximise the views, lounge bar, in-house dining and friendly staff.
